    We Are Playstation

    Playstation has driven innovation forward in the gaming space since 1994.

    The PlayStation family of products and services include PlayStation®5, PlayStation®4, PlayStation®VR, PlayStation™Store, PlayStation®Plus and acclaimed PlayStation software titles from PlayStation Studios.

    The Father of Playstation

    The original playstation was the brainchild of Sony executive Ken Kutaragi. Before the playstation Sony Entertainment Group had limited exposure in the gaming industry. Sony got into the gaming industry by supplying parts for Nintendo consoles, but after Nintendo seperated there partnership in 1991 to go with Phillips instead. The breaking of the partnership infuriated Sony President Norio Ohga, who responded by appointing Kutaragi with the responsibility of developing the PlayStation project to rival Nintendo.
